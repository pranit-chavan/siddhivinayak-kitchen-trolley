Project Quotation & Estimation Analysis

Project: Siddhivinayak Kitchen Trolley ERP & Customer Tracker

Estimation Methodology: Applied COCOMO (Constructive Cost Model)

1. Scientific Estimation Modeling (COCOMO)

To accurately determine the fair market value and required development schedule for this full-stack enterprise platform, the Basic Organic COCOMO Model was utilized and adapted for actual execution constraints.

Execution Constraints:
• Team Size: 1 Full-Stack Developer
• Target Duration: 4 Months

Derived Effort Formula:
Effort (E) = Team Size × Duration = 1 × 4 = 4 Person-Months (PM)

Using the COCOMO Time calculation formula (Tdev = 2.5 × E^0.38), an effort of 4 Person-Months strictly maps to a mathematical development timeline of 4.22 months. This scientifically validates that the planned 4-month lifecycle for this application is accurate and realistic.

2. Commercial Valuation Strategy

COCOMO extracts raw "Effort" (4 Person-Months). To derive the financial quotation, this effort is multiplied by the average commercial billing rate for an independent, skilled full-stack developer in the Indian market.

  • Skillset Required: React, Three.js (WebGL), NestJS, PostgreSQL, Prisma ORM.
  • Fair Market Rate (Solo Freelancer): ₹70,000 per month.
  • Calculation: 4 Person-Months × ₹70,000 = ₹2,80,000
